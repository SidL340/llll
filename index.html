Our Vision

At Shree Nepal Secondary School, we envision a learning community where every student is inspired to reach their full potential. We strive to cultivate a nurturing environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking skills. Our aim is to empower students to become lifelong learners, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens.

About Us

Shree Nepal Secondary School is a co-educational institution that offers classes from 1 to 12. Our school is located in Brindaban, Rautahat, and is committed to providing a holistic education that prepares students for success in all aspects of life.

Academics

We offer a wide range of academic programs, including Science, Management, and Humanities, from class 1 to 12. Our experienced faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure ensure that our students receive a world-class education.
